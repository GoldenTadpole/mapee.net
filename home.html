What is Mapee?

Mapee is a free open-source software, that let's you render a 2D top-down image of your Minecraft worlds. It's a fast and easy to use software that works on Windows 10 and 11 machines.

You can easily switch dimensions. Mapee also has the the ability to filter certain blocks, revealing interesting block formations. It contains 15 styles, a slime chunk viewer, night mode, a tool to measure distances in the world, etc.
